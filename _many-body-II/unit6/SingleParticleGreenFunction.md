The single particle Green function is 

$$\mathcal{G}(\k,ik_0) = \left<\bar{\psi}_{k\sigma} \psi_{k\sigma} \right> = \frac{ \left<\bar{\psi}_{k\sigma} \psi_{k\sigma} e^{-S_\text{int}}\right>}{\left<e^{-S_\text{int}}\right>}$$
